Copayment insight

My Insight Copayment in Thailand is a game-changer for expats and locals buying private insurance. It’s a practical way to keep premiums down—vital in a country where private healthcare is world-class but pricey (e.g., a Bangkok hospital MRI might cost ฿20,000+). However, it’s a double-edged sword: a 20% copay on a ฿500,000 cancer treatment is ฿100,000 out of pocket, which could hit hard. If you’re considering it, weigh your health needs and financial cushion.
